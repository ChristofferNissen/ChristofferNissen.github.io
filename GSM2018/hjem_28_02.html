WORKSHOP DESCRIPTION

1. Win tomorrow. Dare to choose today based on a ‘market fit’ approach.

With increasing market complexity and a growing portfolio choices made today will be critical for our chances to win tomorrow.

You will be exposed to business scenarios, having to choose and prioritise a ‘market fit’ approach to maximize your business.

You will be equipped with insights into what market dynamics and access conditions to consider, when balancing go-to-market strategy and resources.

2. Maximise Patient Access by leveraging Real-World-Evidence and Innovative Contracting

By joining this exciting hands-on workshop you will learn how to best leverage real-world-evidence and innovative contracting to maximise patient access by addressing payer concerns to the clinical data and real world effectiveness of our products.

Participants will be exposed to the key concepts and make strategic choices using real-world-evidence and innovative contracting. Strong collaboration between commercial and medical functions is essential to maximise patient access.

It’s crucial we leverage new concepts and pathways to demonstrate the “real” value of our products. Join us in working successfully towards bending the diabetes curve and improving the health of patients.

3. Creating business impact through Public Affairs

Every day we are working towards ensuring people with diabetes and other chronic diseases have access to the best products and the best possible healthcare. And every day we meet a number of barriers in reaching this goal: Health system barriers, market access barriers, increasing distrust in our industry and crowded competitor landscapes.

In this workshop you will understand how Public Affairs has the potential to be a leading innovative force within your business to help you overcome these barriers.

4. From seed to forest – Expand tomorrow. Today.

Get ready to further expand the obesity market and deliver on the 2019 Global Brand Strategy and market development ambition.

Our strategy has enabled us to plant a lot of seeds in a very small obesity market. Saxenda® is performing well and we now have an exceptional patch of grass, however one cannot forget that, this patch of grass is still quite small, so we need to prioritise our opportunities to expand the patch that will lay the groundwork for tomorrow’s forest.

You are invited to participate in fitting market development initiatives to their specific markets in order to achieve our 2019 Saxenda® ambition.

5. Set the trajectory for Fiasp®, Ryzodeg® and Xultophy® to win tomorrow. Today

Strategy is important but knowing what works and how to execute is critical, join us to find out what it takes to win Tomorrow Today

  • Get to grips with 2019 strategies and tactics
  • Understand what it takes to be successful
  • Get inspired by affiliates sharing practical insights.
6. Digital Health & Patient Support Programme

- Innovating beyond the product through patient centred initiatives

Digital Health and the Patient Support Programme are taking first steps towards driving commercial innovation with the patient at the centre and preparing for the changing landscape ahead.

Through an engaging workshop you will be informed of current Digital Health and Patient Support Programme initiatives, get up to speed with the Digital Health and Patient Support Programme strategies and activate a launch mind-set through an interactive go-to-market game.

Join us and learn more about how we will bring patient centred initiatives to market in the near future!

7. Transform T2D tomorrow, leveraging CV today

To transform the way T2D is treated tomorrow we need to ensure CV risk reduction is leveraged today. Investing in the CV opportunity will not only pave the way for the future success of our portfolio, but also help expand the GLP-1 market.

This workshop will share learnings from affiliates that have successfully worked towards making CV a prescription driver. Best practices will be shared on: CV market development, cardiologist engagement, clinical guidelines, sustained/improved market access.

Participants will leave inspired about how CV risk reduction can drive competitive growth.

8. Get ready for the unprecedented launch of Ozempic®

Are you ready for the unprecedented launch of Ozempic®? Get into the early launch experiences from Canada and USA, play the “O-GAME” to get more familiar with the brand strategy and how an optimal tactical approach will drive uptake.

9. Lead tomorrow with Tresiba® by making ‘Hypos Matter’ today

2018 will be truly exciting for Tresiba® with the launch of the new strategic communication platform – ‘Hypos Matter’ focused on the reduced risk of hypoglycaemia that Tresiba® provides patients requiring basal insulin.

Are you keen to establish the importance of hypoglycaemia in the minds of physicians, KOLs and payers and make it a key prescription driver and scientific differentiator? At the workshop, you will be able to get a clear understanding of the ‘Hypos Matter’ campaigns (branded and unbranded) and initiatives that will drive differentiation and the importance of hypoglycaemia in your markets.

The workshop will activate a launch mindset and provide you with critical success factors and the confidence to execute the new strategic direction and drive future growth for Tresiba®.
